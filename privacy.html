2. Information We Collect

Within the CRM we store and process the following categories of data:

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted on AWS infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit (TLS 1.2+). Access is controlled via Row-Level Security (RLS) policies and role-based authentication. We enforce HTTPS across all connections.

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Retention

Client and prospect data is retained for as long as the business relationship is active or as required by applicable law. Users may request deletion of their account data by contacting the system administrator.

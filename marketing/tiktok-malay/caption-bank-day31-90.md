# MINGGU 10 — Interview Mastery (Hari 66–72)

## Hari 66 · Interview Mastery #1 — STAR Method
**Format:** Talking head + slide overlay, 30s
**HOOK:** "Interview Mastery Series — 7 hari, 7 framework. Hari 1: STAR method cara betul."
**SOLUTION:** "STAR = Situation, Task, Action, Result. Most people know it. Most people do it wrong.

**Wrong:** 'Dalam situasi di mana saya perlu handle complain customer, saya telah mengambil tindakan untuk...' (terlalu panjang, boring)

**Right:** Keep each component to 1-2 ayat maximum. Total STAR = 45-60 saat.
- S: 'Client besar complaint pasal delay delivery — account RM500k risk.'
- T: 'Aku kena resolve dalam 48 jam.'
- A: 'Aku arrange emergency meeting, offer partial refund, expedite delivery.'
- R: 'Client stay, account preserved, diorang jadi case study for us.'

Nombor dalam R adalah mandatory."
**CTA:** "Practice STAR untuk 1 achievement kau sekarang. 60 saat. Komen kalau done."
**Caption:** STAR method — ramai tahu, ramai buat salah. Cara betul + real example. Komen kalau dah practice 🎯

# Facebook Video — 60-Day Storyboard v2
## Insurance Agent CRM · Malaysia Edition

> **v2 — what changed from v1:** every script cut to 20–35s · your real CRM screen in every video · one campaign keyword (**SISTEM**) · WhatsApp blue-ticks instead of web forms · MDRT instead of "promotions" · failed auto-debit instead of "forgot to renew" · no invented statistics · Agent A is always hardworking (the villain is the method, never the person) · Manglish dialogue · ~1 in 4 videos ends on the wound, unresolved · recurring cast with story arcs that pay off.

---

## THE ONE RULE THAT MATTERS MOST

**Every video shows your real app for 3–5 seconds.** Not stock footage, not a mockup — a screen recording of the actual feature. Replace `[APP]` everywhere with your product name and watermark it on screen. Desire needs an object. Without this, you are advertising every competitor's CRM for free.

---

## FORMAT LADDER (5 formats, rotated — never 3 of the same in a row)

| Code | Format | How to produce | Approx. count |
|---|---|---|---|
| **WA** | WhatsApp-chat-UI story | Jianying chat-bubble template, no actors needed | ~18 |
| **SR** | Real screen recording | Record your CRM with cursor, zoom on the money row | ~9 |
| **TH** | Founder talking head | You, phone camera, phone mic, natural light | ~10 |
| **VN** | Client voice-note UI | WhatsApp voice-note visual + audio (record it yourself, vary your voice/get friends) | ~11 |
| **SS** | Split-screen contrast | The classic — reserved for the big emotional days only | ~12 |

**Why:** 60 identical split-screens = decoded by Day 12, throttled by Facebook. Screens (chats, dashboards) are culture-proof, continuity-proof, and 10x faster than casting stock actors.

---

## LENGTH & RETENTION RULES

- **20–35 seconds. Hard cap.** Voiceover max 70 words.
- Hook lands in the **first frame** — an image already in motion (a phone lighting up at 11.04pm, a drawer closing), not a title card.
- Punchline by ~25s. CTA last.
- **WOUND days:** end on the painful image → hold 2 seconds of silence → one text line → quiet CTA. No proverb. No upbeat music. These are the videos people remember tomorrow.

---

## THE ONE KEYWORD FUNNEL

Every video, same CTA: **Comment "SISTEM" — I'll DM you the 2-minute demo.**

Before Day 1 you must have:
1. A **2-minute demo video** of the app (record once, send forever).
2. **Comment auto-reply** set up (ManyChat/Chatrace or manual at small scale): keyword SISTEM → DM with demo link + "want me to set it up for you? Reply YES."
3. A WhatsApp Business number in your page bio.

One keyword trains your audience like a reflex. 55 different keywords (v1) trains nothing.

- **Founder-recorded (your own voice, phone mic):** all TH days, all WOUND days, all VN client voices. Your Malaysian accent IS the differentiator — an American AI voice reading "Wah, you remember somemore ah?" kills the video.

- **BM + 中文 variant priority** (re-dub these 10 first once English versions prove out): Days 1, 4, 5, 8, 12, 17, 21, 29, 53.

## WEEKLY BATCH PRODUCTION (Sunday, ~3 hours for the week)

1. Record all SR screens for the week in one sitting (15 min).
2. Record all founder VO/TH takes back-to-back (30 min).
3. Build WA/VN chat mockups from your saved Jianying template (60 min).
4. Assemble 7 videos from the saved draft template (60 min).
5. Schedule via Meta Business Suite. Done — the week is safe even on busy days.

**Screen-recording asset kit to capture ONCE before Day 1:** renewal/expiry list · failed-payment (card expiry) alert · birthday poster + WhatsApp share flow · pipeline / warm-lead ranking · morning priority list · days-since-contact list (red rows) · dormant-prospect list · instant client search · client profile with dependants/coverage · Delivery Listing · KPI progress card · team KPI view · follow-up sequence view.

---

## POSTING & MEASUREMENT

- 1/day, 12pm or 7pm. Boost only the organic top performers (RM10–20/day on winners, never on losers).
- Watch **3-second hold rate** and **SISTEM comment count** — not likes.
- Every Sunday: repost the week's best performer as a Story with the poll "Which one are you — the WhatsApp scroller or the system agent?"
- After Day 60: kill the bottom 40, re-run the top 20 with BM/中文 voiceovers.

## HONESTY GUARDRAILS (verified by review — keep these true)

- **WhatsApp is never auto-sent.** [APP] prepares drafts and reminders; the agent always taps send. Scripts are written this way ('taps the ready draft') — record them this way. Never depict the app replying to a brand-new inbound message by itself.
- **Festival wish days (28, 32, 51):** record the client list/tag view your app genuinely has — no invented filter UI.
- **No invented numbers.** The only numbers allowed on screen are your own app data (Day 30 style) or honest observations ('most books I see…').
- **Cast rules the story arcs depend on:** Uncle Roland NEVER uses [APP] (he is the warmly-treated notebook veteran — Days 13, 34, 55). Azlan adopts only in Day 54. Marcus adopts in Day 15. Priya's Day 1 slow reply pays off in Day 53 as 'same agent, different system'.

# DNJ Instagram — 180-Day Content Calendar (Days 61–180)

**Builds on:** `content-calendar-60day.md` (Days 1–60, foundation phase).
**Goal:** 10K → 50K (stretch 100K) followers in 90 more days.
**Voice:** Hidden Talent Movement · gold/black · Hook → Pain → Solve → CTA.
**Framework:** `viral-framework-2026.md` is the law for every post.

## Phase map (Days 61–180)

| Phase | Days | Theme | Goal | Follower target |
|--|--|--|--|--|
| 3 — Authority | 61–90 | Deepen Hiring Truth + Career Reality. First HR-side carousel cluster. Case-study seeding. | Be referenced as the MY hiring authority. | 10K |
| 4 — Distribution | 91–120 | Formalize series (Hiring Truth, Career Reality, Hidden Talent). Lock 5 collab reels. | Grid recognizable from one tile. | 25K |
| 5 — Conversion | 121–150 | Featured roles + DNJ-wins (real or composite). UI demos. WhatsApp lead flow. | Bio → 1K+ DMs to apply. | 50K |
| 6 — Brand IP | 151–180 | Recurring shows: Monday Meme · Wednesday WIN · Friday Reality · weekly Quote. Position for a 12-month brand. | Distinct format → repeatable virality. | 100K stretch |

Cadence per week (every week, Days 61–180): **7 reels + 2 carousels + 1 quote + daily Stories**. Reels = reach; carousels = saves; quote = brand recall.

## Repurpose rules (Phase 4+ onward)

Per Chalette 4-step: *every viral structure is repeatable*. Once we have a winner (>50K reach reel, >500 saves carousel), schedule re-renders **3 weeks apart, new hook, same bones**:

- Best Hidden Talent reel → re-cut with new POV
- Best Hiring Truth carousel → re-render slide 1 with new banner accent + number
- Best DNJ Win → spin off short version (15s) and long version (45s)

**Rule:** never reuse the exact same image asset within 30 days. Re-render with `make_framework_covers.py` + new copy.

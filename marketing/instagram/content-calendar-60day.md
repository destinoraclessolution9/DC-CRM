# DNJ Instagram — 60-Day Content Calendar (Goal: 10K followers / 90 days)

**Account:** @dnj.ai · **Cadence:** 1 main post/day + 2–3 stories/day · **Voice:** Hidden Talent Movement (gold/black brand)

## The growth engine
- **Reels = reach** (5/week minimum). They bring NEW followers. Carousels/quotes build saves + authority.
- **Shareable emotion > corporate.** Relatable career pain gets sent to friends = #1 growth lever.
- **Every Reel:** hook in 1.5s · subtitles always · trending audio · end frame = D&J + diamondandjeweler.com
- **First 60 min of every post:** reply to every comment; reshare to Stories with a poll/question.
- **Pillars:** 40% relatable pain · 30% Hidden Talent · 15% hiring truths · 10% jobs · 5% product.
- **Accelerators if organic stalls:** collab Reels with mid-tier creators (DM list) + RM5–10/day boost on best Reel.

# DNJ Viral Content Framework 2026 (Chalette-adapted)

Source: 小红书疯狂获客实战营 / Chalette 女皇. We adapt her **虚拟服务 (virtual service)** playbook to DNJ's English-language IG audience (Malaysian fresh grads + hiring managers).

DNJ category = **Virtual Service** (AI job matching is not a physical product). Per Chalette: virtual service covers must show **共鸣 + 方法感 + 解决方案** = *resonance + method-feel + solution*. Translation for us: every post must feel like the audience said "yes, that's me" → then give them a **named method** → then a CTA.

---

## 🎯 The 4-step content model (override the old caption rules)

Every post — caption, carousel, reel, story — must answer 4 questions in order:

| Step | Question | DNJ rule |
|--|--|--|
| ① **Topic (选题)** | What would my customer *type into search*? | Open with one search-keyword line in caption + on cover |
| ② **Copy (文案)** | Hook → Pain → Solve → CTA — does each segment land? | 4-segment caption, no fluff |
| ③ **Visual (图文)** | Does the cover make people stop scrolling? | See "Cover formulas" below |
| ④ **Cover (封面)** | Big text, number, high contrast, 3:4 | See "Cover formulas" below |

### Step 1 — Topic selection rule

Stop posting what we want to say. Start posting what the audience **searches**.

Workflow before every post:
1. Open IG search → type a likely query ("interview tips Malaysia", "fresh grad salary KL", "how to negotiate salary Malaysia"). Copy the autocomplete suggestions — those are the real keywords.
2. Sort the top results by saves/likes (proxy: look at posts with 500+ saves visible). Note the **structure** they use (list? before/after? POV?).
3. Copy the structure, not the content. Reuse proven shapes:
   - `X things nobody tells you about [pain]`
   - `5 mistakes keeping you [stuck]`
   - `[Number] [outcome] in [timeframe]` (e.g. "From RM3.5K to RM6K in 6 months")
   - `POV: [oddly specific moment]`

### Step 2 — Caption: the 4-segment formula

Every caption follows **Hook → Pain → Solve → CTA**. Each segment ≤ 2 lines. No filler.

```
[SEO keyword line in italics]    ← line 0, search-bait

HOOK — one line that makes them stop. Use one of the 4 levers:
  · Scarcity     ("90% of fresh grads do this wrong")
  · Low barrier  ("one tweak to your resume, no rewrite")
  · Buzzword     ("the underpaid trap", "ghost interview")
  · Hot trend    ("after the 2026 MOHE grad survey…")

PAIN — name the specific moment they live in.
  "73 applications. 0 replies. You start to think it's you."

SOLVE — give a NAMED method. Numbered if possible.
  "The Anchor Drop: let them name the number first.
   Then counter with your value, not your demand."

CTA — single ask. Share OR save OR comment.
  "📌 Save this before your next salary conversation."
```

### Step 3+4 — Cover formulas (virtual service)

Per Chalette: virtual service covers = **resonance line + method label + solution payoff**. Build covers with **all 4** of these:

| Element | Rule |
|--|--|
| **Big text** | Headline reads at thumbnail size. If you can't read it on a phone in 1m, it's too small. |
| **Number** | At least one number per cover. `5 mistakes`, `RM2K more`, `90 sec`, `48h`. |
| **Contrast** | Black bg with **gold + offwhite** is on-brand BUT looks dim in the feed. **Action:** add 1 high-contrast accent block — red banner, yellow strip, or white card — to break the dim mood. |
| **Ratio** | Carousels: 4:5 (1080×1350). Reels covers: **3:4 (1080×1440 crop of the 1080×1920 reel)**. Square posts: deprecate after Day 40 — 4:5 wins more feed real estate. |

#### Three cover patterns we'll use

1. **Resonance pattern (best for quote posts)**
   - Top line: a feeling everyone in the audience has felt ("I sent 73 resumes. Heard back from 0.")
   - Middle: the reframe ("It's not you. It's the filter.")
   - Bottom: small label ("DNJ — AI finds your top 3")
2. **Method pattern (best for carousels)**
   - Slide 1: search-keyword title + number + "save this"
   - Slides 2-N: one method per slide, each named (e.g. "The Anchor Drop", "The Value Counter")
   - Final slide: brand + CTA
3. **Outcome pattern (best for reels)**
   - 0-2s: result on screen ("From 73 applications to 1 right match")
   - 3s+: how (the method, 3 steps)
   - End: CTA + handle

### Title rules (cover headline)

Chalette's formula for service: **price + service type + location**. Adapted for us:

`[Outcome / Number] + [Job-search method] + [Malaysia / KL]`

Examples:
- ❌ "How to negotiate salary (5 steps)"
- ✅ "+RM800/month: salary negotiation in Malaysia (5 steps)"
- ❌ "Great companies don't find talent. They reveal it."
- ✅ "Why top hires never apply (Malaysia 2026)"

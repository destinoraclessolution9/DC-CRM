## Universal reel rules

1. **First 2 seconds decide everything.** No intro. Hit the hook immediately.
2. **Subtitles on every reel.** 85% of IG viewers watch sound-off.
3. **Vertical only** — 9:16, 1080×1920.
4. **Brand colors:** navy `#111827` / white / indigo accent `#1E2A5E`. No gold. No real diamond photos.
5. **End frame:** always DNJ logo + URL. Always.
6. **CTA in caption AND on screen.** Don't pick one.
7. **Reply to first 10 comments in under 5 min.** Algorithm-critical.
